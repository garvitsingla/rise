Frequently Asked Questions

RISE (Research, Innovation, Society & Entrepreneurship) is IIIT-Bangalore's annual open house event showcasing the institute's research activities, innovations, and collaborations across multiple domains.

RISE 2026 is open to everyone ‚Äî students, researchers, industry professionals, and anyone interested in technology and innovation. Registration is required for entry.

RISE 2026 is held at the IIIT-Bangalore campus, located at 26/C, Electronics City, Hosur Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560100.

RISE 2026 event can be attended by paying a minimal amount and explore the research happening at IIIT Bangalore. However, prior registration is mandatory for entry. Please register using the "Register Now" button on this page.

Poster presentations are currently open for IIIT-B research scholars and students. If you're interested in presenting your research, please contact the organizing committee through the official channels.
